About Our Technology

Revolutionizing bone health monitoring through acoustic technology

The Problem

Why Traditional Methods Fall Short

Traditional bone densitometers are bulky and impractical for space missions where every kilogram matters. Aging populations need accessible, affordable bone health monitoring to prevent osteoporosis-related fractures.

Current limitations:

  • Expensive, stationary equipment
  • Unavailable in remote locations
  • Not suitable for routine home monitoring
  • Too heavy for space missions
Our Solution

Lightweight, Wearable Innovation

We've developed a lightweight, wearable device that uses acoustic technology to assess bone health non-invasively.

Vibration Generation

The device emits low-frequency acoustic waves (0.5-2 MHz) through the skin to the bone. Vibration motors create controlled mechanical oscillations.

2
üìä

Signal Detection

Piezoelectric sensors capture the acoustic response and measure damping factors, detecting how bone absorbs and reflects energy.
